Answers to the most common questions about CaptainXI

🏏 Getting Started
What is CaptainXI?▼
CaptainXI is India's complete cricket tournament management platform. It lets you run IPL-style player auctions, manage live match scoring, schedule tournaments, and handle player registrations — all from your phone.
Is CaptainXI free?▼
Yes! The Gully plan is free forever — you get 1 auction with up to 3 teams and 20 players. Upgrade to Club (₹799/auction) or Pro (₹1,499/auction) for more teams, players, and premium features.
How do I create my first tournament?▼
Sign up at captainxi.com → go to Dashboard → click "Create Auction" → set your tournament name, team count, and player budget → add teams and players in Setup → hit "Go Live" to start the auction.
Do players or captains need to pay for CaptainXI?▼
No. CaptainXI is a tool for organisers. Players pay the registration fee set by you (the organiser), not to CaptainXI. Our plans are only for the person running the auction/tournament.
🔨 Live Auction
How does the live auction work?▼
The organiser controls the auction from their laptop/phone. Each captain joins via a unique bidder link on their phone. The organiser puts up players one by one, and captains tap to bid in real-time. Going Once → Going Twice → SOLD! Just like the IPL.
How do captains join the auction?▼
Share the bidder link with captains via WhatsApp. They open it on their phone, enter their registered mobile number, verify with OTP, and they're in. Each captain sees their budget, team roster, and can bid live.
Can people watch the auction live?▼
Yes! Share the viewer link with anyone. They enter the 6-digit room code and watch the auction live — current player, bids, team rosters, and results all update in real-time.
What if a captain's phone disconnects?▼
No problem. The captain's session is saved. When they reconnect, they rejoin automatically where they left off. The organiser can see which captains are online/offline in the setup page.
📊 Match Scoring
How does ball-by-ball scoring work?▼
Go to Matches → create a match → set Playing XI for both teams → start scoring. Tap buttons for runs (0,1,2,3,4,6), extras (Wide, No Ball, Bye, Leg Bye), and wickets. The scorer handles all cricket rules automatically — free hits, over changes, innings breaks, and match results.
Can spectators see the live score?▼
Yes! Share the scorecard link with anyone. They see the score update ball-by-ball in real-time — no app download needed, just a browser link.
What happens if I close the scorer by mistake?▼
The scorer auto-saves every ball to the cloud. If you refresh or close the page, it resumes exactly where you left off — no data is lost.
💰 Payments & Plans
How do I collect registration fees from players?▼
Enable Razorpay in your auction setup. Players pay when they register through your registration link. Money goes directly to your Razorpay account — CaptainXI doesn't touch it.
Can I upgrade my plan mid-tournament?▼
Yes! Your upgrade takes effect immediately. Any extra team/player limits unlock right away for your current and future auctions.
What happens when my plan expires?▼
Your existing auctions and data remain accessible. You just can't create new auctions beyond free plan limits until you renew.
Is the payment secure?▼
All payments are processed by Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. We support UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets. We never store your card details.
Can I get a refund?▼
Per-auction plans are non-refundable once activated. Annual plans can be refunded within 7 days if no auction has been created on the plan. Contact us via WhatsApp for refund requests.
