Why AuctionXI

Not Just a Tool.
An Experience.

Every feature is designed to make your tournament feel bigger, more professional, and more exciting than anything your players have seen before.

📱
Captains Bid From Their Phones
No laptop needed. Each team captain logs in on their phone with a unique team code and bids live. Real competition, real excitement.
⏱️
Auto Going Once / Twice Timers
30-second Going Once, 10-second Going Twice — automatically triggered after every bid. Resets if someone bids again. Drama built in.
📺
Live Viewer Screen
Share a link with anyone. Friends, family, spectators — they watch the auction live with current player, live bid, and stage timer.
🔒
Secure Session Management
OTP-verified login. One device per team. If someone logs in on another phone, the old session is instantly killed. No cheating.
📋
WhatsApp Team Cards
After the auction, one click copies a formatted team card for each captain — squad, roles, amounts, captain name — ready to paste on WhatsApp.
🎲
Random Next Player
After each sale, the next player comes up randomly and automatically in 7 seconds. Keeps the pace. Keeps the energy.
Simple Setup

Live in 10 Minutes

From zero to full IPL-style live auction in the time it takes to have a chai.

1
Create Auction
Set up your tournament name, teams, budgets, and import your player list via CSV or add manually.
2
Share Team Codes
One click copies a WhatsApp message with every captain's unique code and the bidder link.
3
Go Live
Hit Go Live. Lobby shows you who has joined. When all teams are in, 60-second countdown begins automatically.
4
Bid & Win
Players come up randomly. Captains bid. Timers count down. SOLD auto-executes. Drama guaranteed.
