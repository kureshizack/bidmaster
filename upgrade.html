❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade mid-tournament?▼
Yes! Your upgrade takes effect immediately. Any extra team/player limits unlock right away for your current and future auctions.
What happens when my plan expires?▼
Your existing auctions and data remain accessible. You just can't create new auctions beyond free plan limits until you renew.
Is the payment secure?▼
All payments are processed by Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. We support UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets. We never store your card details.
Can I get a refund?▼
Per-auction plans are non-refundable once activated. Annual plans can be refunded within 7 days if no auction has been created on the plan. Contact support@captainxi.com.
Do players or captains need to pay for CaptainXI?▼
No. CaptainXI is a tool for organisers. Players pay registration fees set by you (the organiser), not to CaptainXI. Our plans are only for the organiser running the auction.
